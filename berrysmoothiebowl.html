Berry Smoothie Bowl
Ingredients
  • ½ cup strawberry
  • ½ cup raspberry
  • 1 cup blackberry
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • ½ cup greek yogurt
  • ¼ cup almond milk, or soy milk
  • ¼ cup peanut butter
Step-By-Step Instructions
  1. Add the berries, banana, Greek yogurt, almond milk, and peanut butter to a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Top with your favorite toppings.
  3. Enjoy!
